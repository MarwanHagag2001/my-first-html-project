In business: The automation of robotic processes is applied to highly repetitive tasks normally carried out by humans. Machine learning algorithms are integrated into analytics and CRM platforms to uncover information on how to serve customers more effectively. Chatbots for delivering immediate customer support have already been integrated into websites and e businesses. Workplace automation has also become a discussion point between academics and IT consultancies.

In Healthcare: Companies apply machine learning to make diagnoses better and faster than people. It understands the natural language and can answer questions that have been put about it. The system exploits patient data and other available data sources to form a hypothesis, which it then presents with a scoring scheme for confidence. AI is a research undertaken to replicate human intelligence through computer technology that could assist patients as well as the doctor.


AI for robotics will enable us to tackle the challenges of caring for an aging population and allow for much longer independence. It will drastically reduce traffic accidents and deaths, as well as allow for disaster response in hazardous situations.
